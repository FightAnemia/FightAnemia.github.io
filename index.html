What is this Project?

Anemia is a common blood related disorder which is caused by the lack of iron in one's bloodstream. It is prevalent in Sri Lanka where a significant portion of women and children are suffering from the disease. Especially in rural areas, testing for anemia can be expensive,logistically compolicated and can cause complications such as infections due to the lack of hygiene when drawing blood.

This project is offering a non-invasive solution to detect anemia with high accuracy while minimizing the adverse effects of traditional methods. Using the system to get results is easy and the procedure is simple enough to be performed by regular people without specialized training. The system is robust enought to withstand many testing instances and is also relative cheap enough that the device can be replaced without much cost. This project utilizes multiple inputs from a user to deliver more accurate results and the cloud nature of technologies and software used in the project means that the system can be scaled as needed without being prohibitively expensive.
